About Apartment550

Apartment550 is a highly regarded, classic hotel ideally situated in the serene environment of Lifecamp, Abuja, the federal capital territory of Nigeria. Our primary focus is on providing exceptional hospitality to all our guests. We offer a range of luxurious, well-furnished rooms catering to various classes, ensuring utmost comfort. Our establishment features a first-class restaurant and bar, an inviting swimming pool, and several relaxation spots, all designed to provide the highest level of comfort and satisfaction for our valued customers.
